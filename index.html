Our Story

Arni Abarana: Where Earth Meets Art

Born from a passion for transforming humble clay into objects of beauty, Arni Abarana represents the union of artistic vision and wearable adornment. Our name reflects this philosophy: 'Arni' for creative essence, 'Abarana' for decorative elegance.

Each earring begins as raw earth, shaped by hand, dried with care, and finished with attention to the smallest detail. We celebrate imperfections as marks of the human hand, making every pair uniquely yours.

Our mission is to create sustainable, lightweight jewelry that doesn't just accessorize, but expresses—connecting the wearer to the timeless art of sculpture.

Our Process

Hand Sculpting

Each design begins as a concept in our studio, shaped carefully by hand from premium clay.

Natural Drying

Pieces are air-dried slowly to preserve integrity, preventing cracks and ensuring durability.

Artisan Finishing

We apply custom glazes, paints, and finishes, often hand-mixed for unique color variations.

Quality Assembly

Each piece is fitted with hypoallergenic, surgical-grade earring posts and backs for comfort.
